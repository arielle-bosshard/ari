About Me.

I have chosen a lifestyle of travel and expansion, my latest adventure being based around Italy and Switzerland. My journey into tech started with a degree in Arts and Performance. I realised early on that my interests in these fields were in design, organisation and construction. Due to my dreams of living overseas, I looked into possible work solutions that supported my interests and dreams. I found website design and building! It let me play to my strengths that I found in theatre and have the freedom to travel and connect with people across the world. I have found a passion in front-end development where I can use my design and organisation skills to write clean, classy code.
